Privacy Policy for Chibi on Track

Last updated: 2026/07/06

Thank you for using Chibi on Track ("the App," "we," "us," or "our"), provided by Chibi on Track. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information. Please read it carefully.

The short version: The information you record in Chibi on Track (your child's profile, logs, health records, and notes) is stored only on your device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share that personal content. There are no accounts and no servers for your data. The App does, however, display advertising, and our advertising provider (Google) receives limited device information to serve those ads, as described in Section 3.

1. Information stored on your device

The App lets you record information about your child and yourself, which may include:

  • Child profile details (name, nickname, date of birth, gender, weight, height, photo or avatar)
  • Daily logs (feedings, sleep, diaper changes, growth measurements, notes)
  • Health records you choose to enter (vaccines, appointments, health incidents, photos)
  • Parent information (name, avatar, wellbeing check-ins, tasks, contacts, journal entries)
  • App preferences (theme, measurement units)

All of this information is stored only on your device, using local storage on your phone. It is never sent to us or to any third party. We have no ability to access, view, or retrieve it.

2. Information we do not collect ourselves

We do not collect, use, or store the personal content you enter into the App. We have no user accounts, no servers that receive your data, and no analytics that track your identity. We do not sell any information.

3. Advertising

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob (Google LLC). To serve these ads, Google may collect and process limited information from your device, which can include:

  • Device identifiers (such as an advertising identifier)
  • General device and technical information (such as device type, operating system, and approximate, non-precise location derived from your IP address)
  • Information about ad interactions (such as whether an ad was shown or tapped)

We use non-personalized ads. This means ads are served based on general, contextual information rather than your past behavior or interests, and Google is instructed not to use your data to build an advertising profile of you for personalization within the App. Even so, Google may still process limited device data as described above, for purposes such as serving ads, frequency capping, aggregate reporting, and fraud and abuse prevention.

This device information is collected and processed by Google, not by us. We do not receive the personal content you enter into the App, and that content is never shared with Google or used for advertising. To learn how Google handles data, see Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy and "How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services" at https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

4. Photos

If you choose to add a photo (for a profile picture or a health incident), the App accesses your photo library only to let you select an image. The selected image is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded anywhere. The App does not use your camera and does not access photos without your action. Photos are never shared with our advertising provider.

5. Contacting us

If you choose to contact us directly (for example, by emailing us with feedback or a support request), we will receive the information you send us, such as your email address and the contents of your message. We use this only to respond to your inquiry.

6. Data storage, backup, and deletion

  • Your data lives on your device. If you delete the App, the data stored within it is removed.
  • Your device's own backup system (for example, iCloud device backups) may include the App's data as part of a full-device backup, according to your device settings. This is controlled by you and your device, not by us.
  • You can erase all data from within the App at any time using the Reset all data option in Settings.
  • You can limit ad tracking and reset your advertising identifier at any time in your device settings (on iOS: Settings › Privacy & Security › Tracking, and Settings › Privacy & Security › Apple Advertising).

7. Children's privacy

Chibi on Track is a tool intended for use by parents and caregivers, not by children. While the App is used to record information about a child, that information stays on the caregiver's device and is not collected by us. The App is not directed to children, is not intended for the Apple "Kids" category, and does not use behaviorally targeted advertising. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Security

The personal content you enter never leaves your device, so its security depends primarily on your device's own protections (such as your passcode or biometric lock). We recommend keeping your device secured and up to date.

9. Revisions to this policy

If the App changes in a way that affects how information is handled, we will update this Privacy Policy and revise the "Last updated" date above. Any important revision, together with the latest version of the Privacy Policy, will be posted on our website or within the App. The revised version becomes effective and applies to users at the time it is posted.

10. Limitation of liability

To the maximum extent permitted by law, Chibi on Track shall not be liable for any damages of any kind arising from the use of the App.
